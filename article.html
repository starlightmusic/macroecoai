See how our AI-driven analysis breaks down complex topics

What is Inflation?

Inflation represents the rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services rises, eroding purchasing power over time. Central banks monitor inflation to maintain economic stability.

Causes of Inflation

Common causes include demand-pull conditions when demand outpaces supply, cost-push factors like rising production costs, and built-in inflation expectations within the economy.
